Summer Cosplay Celebration

FAQ

  1. How do I get there?
    See the instructions from the Arts at the Armory website:http://artsatthearmory.org/about/getting-here/. More specifically, there are 88 buses on August 1 leaving from Davis Square every 20 minutes starting at 6:03pm.
  2. Is it a kid-friendly event?
    All of our dance performances have been asked to be kid-friendly and one of our performance groups is a kids troupe. We ask cosplayers to please keep their attire kid-friendly as well (see below).
  3. What is the dress-code for cosplayers?
    Cosplay outfits should be inspired by an original manga, anime, and/or video game. Please leave the stormtroopers, hobbits, and other popular costumes at home for another time!
    Please refrain from overly skimpy/revealing outfits, offensive writing/attire, culturally offensive attire (i.e. blackface, swastikas), fake blood, sharp edges, real weapons, oversized, overly complicated, and anything that might cause damage to others. When in doubt, please feel free to e-mail penumbra.rme@gmail.com
  4. Will there be refreshments?
    Arts at the Armory will have a cash bar setup where you can buy drinks during the event. Soft drinks range from $2-2.50, beer ranges from $3-13 (most are about $5), and wine by the glass ranges $6-7.50. There may also be some free candy to nibble on!
  5. What if I want to play the raffle but I'm not wearing a costume?
    We may offer the option to buy a raffle ticket, details TBD!
