Summer Cosplay Celebration

Saturday, August 1

6:30 pm

Arts at the Armory

Come show off your favorite anime/manga or video game cosplay outfit, admire the creations of others, and enjoy live performances from local Boston dance companies. All attendees who show up in cosplay are entered into a raffle for prizes from Demiurge Studios, other local businesses, and old school collectibles. This an all-ages event with doors opening at 6:30, performances at 7:30, and the raffle around 8:30.
