Summer Cosplay Celebration

Saturday, August 1

6:30 pm

Arts at the Armory
191 Highland Ave
Somerville, MA

Come show off your favorite anime/manga or video game cosplay outfit, admire the creations of others, and enjoy live performances from local Boston dance companies. This an all-ages event with doors opening at 6:30, performances at 7:30, and the raffle around 8:30. Tickets available now at http://penumbrarme.yapsody.com
